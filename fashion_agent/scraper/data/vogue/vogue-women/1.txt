Khushi Kapoor's corset and cargos combo proves her cool-girl status
To revisit this article, visit My Profile, then View saved storiesTo revisit this article, visit My Profile, then View saved storiesBy Sanaa SharmaKhushi Kapoor, with her baggy pants, shoulder bags, and half-zip sweaters, has always epitomised Gen-Z style. Somehow, she always manages to toe the line between casual and glamorous with her outfits—and her recent look, which involved baggy cargo pants and a corset top, is a masterclass in how to do so.This content can also be viewed on the site it originates from.Styled by Mohit Rai, the actress was photographed wearing a sage green corset from Munique, a brand that prides itself on creating “urban couture”. She paired the corset with baggy cargo pants from Polite Society and wore a pair of beige stilettos to complement the look. Her styling was minimal as she opted for stacked bracelets, matte makeup, a nude lip, and freshly blown-out hair to finish the look off.The death of the late, great Vivienne Westwood and the popularity of Regency-era drama Bridgerton were some of the many factors that led to the corset’s sudden, post-pandemic popularity. But, really, the corset’s staying power lies in its versatility, and how easily a corset look can go from dressed-up to dressed-down with appropriate styling.
