The key to a crochet dress is the styling: Panday went casual as she opted for a dainty necklace and aviator sunglasses.Crochet is at its most interesting when used as a layering tool: Chloé opted for neutral dresses under crochet in SS22 while celebrity proponents like Dua Lipa pair it with jeans.
