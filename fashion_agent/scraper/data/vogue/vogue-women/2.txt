Priyanka Chopra proves that the cropped jacket is the ultimate concert staple
To revisit this article, visit My Profile, then View saved storiesTo revisit this article, visit My Profile, then View saved storiesBy Sanaa SharmaPriyanka Chopra Jonas has never shied away from outfits that involve her take on a trend: Barbiecore, feathers, animal prints—you name it, she’s done it. She recently wore an outfit with her take on a trend that's been picking up steam: the small, cropped jacket, spotted on the likes of Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber after dominating runways in the winter.This content can also be viewed on the site it originates from.The actress was photographed at a Jonas Brothers show wearing an outfit that consisted of a midi skirt from Bulgari and a racecar check-patterned bralette, on top of which she wore a baggy, glossy, cropped shacket, left open. Chopra paired the outfit with strappy black heels and silver jewellery and carried Bulgari’s Serpenti shoulder bag, all while sporting her signature red lip.Outerwear has always evaded the midriff-baring movement—till now. As skirt and pant hemlines are falling down, jackets and coats are going up: the runways during New York Fashion Week were replete with mini leather jackets, small blazers, and shortened shearlings.
