Best fashion Instagrams of the week—celebrities embraced saris to mermaid-style dresses from Suhana Khan to Kiara Advani
To revisit this article, visit My Profile, then View saved storiesTo revisit this article, visit My Profile, then View saved storiesBy Piya DhimanAs the curtains rise on a new fashion season, we anticipate an onset of the fresh wedding guest style dropping aplenty. Our best-dressed celebrities including Kiara Advani and more, have already kicked off the season with show-stopping ethnic ensembles. Be it Manish Malhotra’s signature chiffon saris taking the internet and the fashion world by storm or Gen Z star kids turning to saree looks with barely-there blouses.Alia Bhatt adorned yet another Manish Malhotra sari, this time in an electric rani pink shade blending chiffon with a velvet blouse, proving pink-on-pink action will always steal the moment, after all, how much pink is too much pink. Suhana Khan also gave her stamp of approval to the wave of saris this week, as she was also spotted in a Bollywood-inspired electric blue sari designed by Arpita Mehta.There was no dearth of traditional ensembles this week, coming from the embellished saris of Khushi Kapoor and Karisma Kapoor vouching for handloom saris with her latest post. While ethnic glamour reigned supreme, celebrities like Kiara Advani and Sobhita Dhulipala gave coral-inspired Amphitrite-esque looks.Advani came decked in a breathtaking off-shoulder gown designed by the duo Gauri & Nainika. The gown featured intricate corset detailing and bodycon patterns that extended down to the waist, before transitioning into a flowing skirt which cascaded to the floor, culminating in a sweeping train.Basking in the limelight of the much-anticipated release of the new season of Made in Heaven, actress Sobhita Dhulipala graced the series’ premier with a striking appearance. She chose to wear an attention-grabbing asymmetrical gown adorned with captivating 3-D textures. The gown featured transparent netted details at the torso, accompanied by a dramatic halter neckline formed by a black netted bodice.And finally, proving that classics never go out of style, Neena Gupta proved that LBDs and her sartorial sense have no expiration date. Once again defying all notions of age-appropriate dressing, Neena Gupta gauged all attention by stepping out in a little black dress with thin spaghetti straps skilfully paired with thigh-high black suede boots. On the contrary, the ultimate queen of fashion's biggest pop culture moments, Kareena Kapoor Khan posted her rainy day outfit– a chic white slit dress.
