The Met Gala 2023 is upon us. And the best-dressed men have arrived.Held at New York City's Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City, it's fashion's biggest night out – and a VIP-heavy fundraiser for the Costume Institute. And, on its 75th year, the theme – an annual dress code that serves as a reflection of the fashion landscape – pays homage to a late titan of the industry: "Karl Lagerfeld: A Line of Beauty”.As one of the few Met Galas that pays tribute to a specific individual as opposed to a wider moment, those in attendance didn't hold back. Lots of really, really big fits. Massive serves. Ample opportunity for Fashion Twitter to dissect and discuss as appropriate.Here are all the best-dressed men from the Met Gala 2023.
